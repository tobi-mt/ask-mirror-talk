## What Is Ask Mirror Talk?

Ask Mirror Talk is an AI-powered companion for the **Mirror Talk podcast** — a faith-based, personal-growth podcast covering emotional intelligence, relationships, healing, and purpose.

**How it works:**
- Ask questions in plain language about any topic the podcast has covered
- Receive warm, conversational answers synthesized from real podcast episodes
- Each answer is linked directly to the moments in the audio where the topic was discussed
- Explore by topic, discover curated questions, and build a daily habit

## Core Features

### 🤖 Ask a Question

**How to use:**
1. Type your question in the text box labeled "What's on your heart?" (up to 500 characters)
2. Press **Ask** or use **Ctrl+Enter** (Windows) / **Cmd+Enter** (Mac)
3. Watch as the answer streams in word-by-word in real time
4. A 3–4 paragraph conversational response appears, synthesized from relevant podcast episodes

**Conversation context:**
- The app remembers the last 3 exchanges in your session
- Follow-up questions can reference what was said earlier
- Perfect for diving deeper into a topic

**Example questions:**
- "How do I find peace when I'm anxious?"
- "What does forgiveness really mean?"
- "How can I build healthier boundaries?"

### 📍 Referenced Episodes (Citations)

Every answer includes **Referenced Episodes** — the exact moments in podcast episodes that informed the response.

Each citation card shows:
- **Episode title and year** — helps you identify relevant episodes
- **Quote snippet** — a relevant quote from that segment
- **Timestamp range** — exactly where to listen (e.g., "14:22 – 17:45")

**Click any citation to:**
- Open an inline audio player
- Jump directly to the cited moment and auto-play
- Hear the context in the speaker's own voice
- Auto-pause at the end of the cited segment

**Player controls:**
- ⏮ Back 10 seconds
- ⏭ Forward 10 seconds
- 📤 Open in new tab (full episode)
- ✕ Close player

**Tip:** Only one player is open at a time — opening a second automatically closes the first.

## PWA Installation

### 💻 Install as App on Your Device

Ask Mirror Talk works as a Progressive Web App (PWA), meaning it's installable on mobile, tablet, and desktop.

**Why install?**
- 🚀 Launches instantly like a native app
- 🌐 Works offline with cached content
- 📲 Adds icon to home screen
- 🔔 Sends push notifications
- 💾 Full access to saved insights and reflections

**How to install:**

#### Android (Chrome)
1. Open Ask Mirror Talk in Chrome
2. Tap the **three-dot menu** (top right)
3. Tap **"Add to Home Screen"**
4. Confirm the app name and tap **"Install"**

**OR:** Wait for the install prompt to appear automatically

#### iOS (Safari)
1. Open Ask Mirror Talk in Safari
2. Tap the **Share button** (bottom center)
3. Scroll and tap **"Add to Home Screen"**
4. Confirm the app name and tap **"Add"**

**OR:** In newer iOS versions, wait for an automatic install prompt

#### Desktop (Chrome/Edge)
1. Open Ask Mirror Talk in your browser
2. Click the **install icon in the address bar** (looks like a computer with an arrow)
3. Confirm the app name and click **"Install"**

**After installation:**
- The app appears on your home screen as "Ask Mirror Talk"
- Open it anytime like any other app
- Enjoy offline access and full features

## Troubleshooting

### Can't see notifications?
- Ensure notifications are enabled in the widget (🔔 icon)
- On mobile: check your device's notification settings for the browser/app
- On desktop: check browser notification permissions

### Can't install as PWA?
- Ensure you're visiting over HTTPS (secure connection)
- Close the browser and try again
- Try a different browser if available
- PWA installation not supported in all browsers

### Answer seems incomplete or wrong?
- Try a more specific question
- Click 👎 and leave feedback so we improve
- Try related follow-up questions to refine your search

### Saved insights disappeared?
- Saved insights are stored on your device
- Clearing browser data will erase them
- Sync across devices not yet supported

### Streak reset when it shouldn't have?
- Ensure you asked a question that day (ask the Daily QOTD)
- Check timezone settings if traveling
- Contacts support if issue persists
